About Me

An Idealist

Inspiration

I was first introduced to web design when my dad and I worked on the website for our unicycling club. Since then, web design became my hobby. After improving my skills, I volunteered and made websites for an open source computer board company because I thought their existing websites were not up to date with the new material / flat design trends.

In addition to my exposure to web design, I became interested in brain machine interfaces after reading various sci-fi light novels. The light novels conceptualized a device that can push sensory data to the brain and connect users online in a virtual reality world.

Along with my interests, I also have a dream, the dream of achievement. During my years in high school, I attempted crowdfunding projects, math and physics competitions, programming events, but I never achieved my goal in any of them. All of my failed attempts eroded away my ego, but my passion to create is still burning inside. I, as a nonentity, am presenting to you my latest project with only two things, a lot of passion and a little hope. Please enjoy my fusion of interactive websites with a brain machine interface.

Background

My name is Zhizhuo. I am a highschool student bearing the responsibility of learning at school with a feeling that I can work on more interesting projects during my spare time. I am attending UCVTS in New Jersey and studying a math, physics, and engineering heavy curriculum.
